Welcome to Folks website!

This is a website designed for older people, to allow you to interact with the internet with a minimum of fuss. We have endeavoured to create a site that is simple and easy to use, that will become a useful tool for you, adding to your daily experience.

At the top of the page is a permanent "Navigation" bar that you can click on that will allow you to select things on the website. We have placed the Radio link in the middle so that you always know where to find it, to the left of that is located the News and Academic links and to the right the Services and Communications links.

Should you have any queries or suggestions please do not hesitate to contact us by clicking the contact link at the bottom of the page and filling out the form that appears.

We hope you find this site to your liking and bookmark us for future reference!
